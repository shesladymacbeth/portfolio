About Me

Currently residing in Portland, OR with my wife, I work remotely for an independent marketing firm as a Copywriter and Marketing Coordinator. Constantly in the company of my two hairless cats, I am a self-starter who truly enjoys the company of others. I excel in my client relations role because I find joy in each conversation and strive to find the good in every situation, no matter how stressful. I’ve spent my time at Good Things Done Right maximizing my ability to produce content for clients ranging anything from non-profits, to hospitals, to wineries. Regardless of the topic, rest assured I can read up, learn quickly, and run with it with enthusiasm.
